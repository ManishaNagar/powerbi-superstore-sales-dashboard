# 📊 Super Store Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

This project presents an interactive **Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI** to analyze retail sales performance, customer segments, regional trends, and future sales forecasts.

The goal of this project is to transform raw retail data into **actionable business insights** that help understand sales patterns and support data-driven decision making.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

The dashboard is divided into **two main analytical pages**:

### 1️⃣ Sales Performance Dashboard
This page provides an overview of the company’s sales performance across multiple business dimensions including:

- Product Categories
- Sub-Categories
- Customer Segments
- Payment Modes
- Shipping Methods
- Geographic Regions
- Monthly Sales Trends

### 2️⃣ Sales Forecast Dashboard
This page focuses on **predictive insights**, where historical sales data is used to forecast **future sales trends for the next 15 days**.

This helps businesses anticipate demand and plan inventory, logistics, and marketing strategies accordingly.

---

# 📈 Key Business Insights

- The store generated **$1.57M in total sales** with **22K products sold** and **$175K total profit**.

- **Office Supplies ($0.64M)** is the top-selling category, followed by **Technology ($0.47M)** and **Furniture ($0.45M)**.

- At the product level, **Phones ($0.20M)** generate the highest revenue, followed by **Chairs ($0.18M)** and **Binders ($0.17M)**.

- The **Consumer segment contributes 48% of total sales**, making it the largest customer segment.

- **Online payments (43%)** are the most preferred payment method compared to Card and Cash on Delivery.

- **Standard shipping accounts for the majority of orders ($0.91M)**, suggesting customers prioritize lower delivery cost over faster shipping.

- **East (33%) and West (29%) regions generate the highest sales**, indicating strong market demand in these areas.

- **California leads with $0.34M in total sales**, making it the highest-performing state.

- Forecasting analysis predicts **future sales trends for the next 15 days**, enabling better business planning.
